EASY ENGLISH’s Head Teacher Kyle Isa Holds BA in English and Literature from Bishkek Humanities University (Kyrgyzstan, 1998), MS in International Studies from Oklahoma State University (the USA, 2011), and Certificates in Entrepreneurship from Israel and Business and Management from Japan. He has 25 years of experience in the education sector, including teaching English to adults and young learners; taught Talking English in Turkey, the United States, Kyrgyzstan. He has also been a tutor of TOEFL and GRE. Recently, Kyle taught English at JustEnglish, a dynamically growing language school in Istanbul. Currently, he teaches English online to English learners in the United States, Russia, Turkey, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, and Ukraine. In teaching English, Kyle focuses on developing imaginative abilities which allow students to start thinking in English without translating their thoughts from their native into English.

Successfully implemented projects:
• Improvement of lives of the people in underprivileged new settlings around Bishkek through teaching them basic English and computer skills, 2013-2015.
• Improvement of lives of the vulnerable women in Bishkek through teaching them basic English and computer skills, 2016-2018.
• Contribution to the development of tourism through teaching long-distance taxi drivers in basic English, 2017-2019.
Kyle speaks English, Japanese, Russian, Kyrgyz, and he understands well all Turkic group languages. EASY ENGLISH is suitable for students whose native or first language is one of the above mentioned.

Mission statement

To help our students make positive changes in their communities and around the world through effective communication. We believe that irrespective of age, race, gender, social status, or occupation, people can learn English and use it in their everyday life.

Goal

EASY ENGLISH is a dynamically growing online program. Our program focuses on improving listening comprehension, pronunciation, grammar, and reading.

With these and other attempts, we help our students make their English sound close to the native one, so it allows our students to effectively communicate with native speakers of English and English-speaking communities around the globe. Although our tutors teach from the United States and Kyrgyzstan, our students can learn English with us from any country in the world. Currently, we have students based in Turkey, the United States, Ukraine, Russia, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan who learn English online with our program.
