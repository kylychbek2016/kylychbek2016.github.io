Mission statement

To help our students be able to make positive changes in their communities and around the world through effective communication. We believe that anyone irrespective of age, race, gender, social status or occupation, is able to learn English and use it in their everyday life.

Goal

EASY ENGLISH is a dynamically growing online program. Our program is specifically focused on improving listening comprehension, pronunciation, grammar and reading.

With these and other attempts we help our students make their English sound close to native one, so this allows our students to effectively communicate with native speakers of English and English speaking communities around the globe. Although our tutors teach from the United States and Kyrgyzstan, our students can learn English with us from any country around the world. Currently, we have students based in Turkey, the United States, Ukraine, Russia, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan who learn English online with our program.
